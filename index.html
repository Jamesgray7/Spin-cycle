What else we have to offer

  • A Laundrette with style

    We've worked hard on the design and layout of the venue. there's hidden details everywhere you look and the majority of our furniture is custom made.

  • Free internet access

    Our customers are treated to high-speed internet access at no extra cost. Just check the receipt for the password.

  • An alternative drink selection

    Whilst the cafe is open every day, if you happen to catch one of our live music nights, watch our cafe transform into a vibrant bar with a wide array of drinks.

  • Music for everyone

    We try to cater our music to appeal to all of our customers, although we do really like Hip-Hop. We host a variety of different music events, so check out the Instagram to see whats upcoming.

  • Community

    Unlike your typical, anti-social laundrette, we try to connect people through the music, as well as, the ability to sit and chat in our spacious seating area.

  • Don't kill the vibe, man

    Here at Spin Cycle we have a zero anti social behaviour and drug tolerance policy. So don't get caught or you'll be asked to leave.
